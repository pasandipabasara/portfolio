About Me

Full Stack Developer!

Hello! My name is Pasandi Pabasara, and I'm a web designer from Sri Lanka, learning through the computer science stream.

I have over 3 years of experience in web design. My main focus is creating websites that look great and are easy for people to use. I make sure to keep up with the latest design trends and use my creativity to build visually attractive and user-friendly websites.

I understand how important it is to make websites simple and enjoyable for users. I always put myself in the users' shoes to ensure they have a smooth experience. For every new project, I carefully research the audience and listen to the client’s needs. This helps me create designs that match what they’re looking for and provide a unique online presence that fits their brand.
